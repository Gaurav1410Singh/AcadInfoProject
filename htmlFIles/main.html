About the AcadInfo

AcadInfo is a centralized, student-friendly platform designed exclusively for the students of Rajkiya Engineering College Bijnor. It serves as a one-stop solution for accessing all essential academic information in a streamlined and organized manner. From AKTU semester results and university guidelines to academic notices, curriculum details, and scholarship information — everything is now just a click away.

The goal of AcadInfo is to eliminate confusion and save time for students by providing verified, up-to-date resources on a single platform. Whether you're looking for your latest university results, important notices, or need to check your syllabus, AcadInfo simplifies it all.

This platform has been passionately developed by Gaurav Singh, a B.Tech student from the Information Technology Department at Rajkiya Engineering College Bijnor. With a keen interest in software development, he created this project to support his peers and enhance access to academic information using clean design and robust functionality.
